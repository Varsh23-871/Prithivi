Prithvi Hall


Prithvi Hall is an ideal hosting venue for all kinds of events like Weddings, Pre and Post Wedding Functions, Receptions, Betrothal, Baby shower, Upanayanam, birthday parties etc. The venue is properly equipped with traditional and modern amenities and is embelished with attractive interiors including good lighting, chandeliers and ...audio system. The hall has a seating capacity of 170 people and floating capacity of 300 people. The hall is located in close proximity to the bus terminus, railway station and market. The place is very well connected through chennai by-pass road and is also near to Padi, Anna Nagar, Surapet, Mogappair, Ayapakkam and Korattur.
